Who We Are

Maktoub is more than apparel.It's meaning, emotion, and storytelling stitched into every piece.

Why Maktoub?

Maktoub translates to "it is written" a reminder that your path, your moments, and your identity are uniquely yours. Our brand celebrates these chapters through minimal, timeless pieces.

Our Vision

To create apparel that feels personal. Pieces that feel like home. Soft textures, calm tones, and pieces that stay with you. We design with intention, emotion, and storytelling at the core.

Our Values

  • High quality fabrics
  • Minimal + meaningful design
  • Seasonal drops inspired by culture
  • Slow fashion approach
